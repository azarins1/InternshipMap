Tech Internships in United States

Arthur Zarins

This study visualizes the distribution of technology internships across the United States.

One of the most popular job boards for tech jobs is Simplify's annual internship list, with over 40,000 stars on GitHub. Over 7,000 archive internship posts for 2026 with role and location information from Simplify were used as the dataset for this study. Internship posts that didn't specify a city location would count towards a state's total internship count but were not plotted on the map. Cities were convereted to latitude and longitude coordinates with SimpleMaps's World Cities Database.

Within the dataset, California is the state with the most internship roles in software engineering, hardware, and data science while New York leads in quantitative finance. A limitation of this study is that it counts the number of internship postings while not taking into account that different internship roles will have a differing amounts of interns.
